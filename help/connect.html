Connecting Your Saber
Connect your saber with a good quality USB cable.

Some USB cables are for charging only.

Windows should make it's device connection noise when the saber is plugged in, Windows 10 already knows about the correct drivers for the saber and should install and commect automatically.

Other versions of Windows may need a CDC Serial driver, you can download one from CDC Serial Driver Installer.

Selecting the Correct Serial/COM Port
Gilthoniel detects and connects to the correct serial port automatically, on the rare occassion that the saber does not connect but you are sure it is the right serial port, press the disconnect/reconnect button once or twice.

After making any changes and sending them to the saber, presing the close button or ending the applications will send a save command to the saber. This is not necessary as the saber saves its setting to permanent memory whenever the USB cable is disconnected.
